About DBAMINHS

Dr. Bonifacio A. Masilungan Integrated National High School, established in 2008, is located in Brgy. Lalayat, San Jose, Batangas. The school offers Junior High (Grades 7-10) and Senior High (Grades 11-12) programs.

📊 Grade Upload System

Upload student grades per quarter using a CSV file

📄 CSV Format Guide:

Row 1: Header → LRN,Name,Filipino,English,Math,Science,AP,EsP,TLE,Music & Arts,PE & Health
MAPEH is auto-computed: (Music & Arts + PE & Health) ÷ 2
Row 2+: Data → 136789012345,Juan Dela Cruz,88
Grade: Number from 60-100 only. Save as .csv file from Excel or Google Sheets.

📋 Attendance Upload System

Upload student attendance records using a CSV file

📄 CSV Format:

LRN,Name,Days Present,Days Absent,Days Late,Total School Days
Attendance Rate is auto-computed: (Days Present ÷ Total School Days) × 100%

📅 Class Schedule Upload

Upload class schedule per section using a CSV file

📄 CSV Format:

Day,Time,Subject,Teacher,Room
Example: Monday,7:30-8:30,Filipino,Mrs. Bautista,Room 1
